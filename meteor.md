## [Meteor](https://www.meteor.com/install)

```
curl https://install.meteor.com/ | sh
sudo cp ~/.meteor/packages/meteor-tool/1.4.1_3/mt-os.linux.x86_64/scripts/admin/launch-meteor /usr/local/bin/meteor
```
